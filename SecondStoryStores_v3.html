Pricing principles
The Grey Goddess premium. Story-led listings command 30–50% above generic vintage. Your descriptions are your pricing power — use them fully.
Condition guides price. A+ and A grade → top of range. B grade → middle. C grade → lower end, but still price with confidence.
Psychological pricing. Always end in 0 or 5. R480 sells faster than R475. R550 feels intentional. R523 feels uncertain.
Margin targets. Minimum: 60%. Target: 75%+. Sweet spot: 80%+ (achieved when sourcing cost is R0–R80).
Margin % = (Selling price − Cost price) ÷ Selling price × 100
e.g. Sold R480, cost R80 → (480−80)÷480×100 = 83% margin ✓
